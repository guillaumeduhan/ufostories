*Shag Harbour UFO Incident 1967 — Nova Scotia, Canada*

On October 4, 1967, multiple witnesses in Shag Harbour, Nova Scotia, reported a low-flying object that crashed into the harbour. Royal Canadian Mounted Police (RCMP) officers arrived on the scene to see lights submerged underwater.

An official government-led underwater search commenced, but no wreckage or explanation was ever recovered. The case is notable for its official documentation and acknowledgement as a "UFO."

Shag Harbour remains one of Canada’s most famous UFO incidents, with ongoing interest from researchers and the public.
